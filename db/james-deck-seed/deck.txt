Star Trek Trivia

Description: "Trivia from TOS, TNG, Voyager, DS9 and Enterprise"

1. What was the first episiode of the original series of Star Trek?

Answer: The Cage

2. Actor Tim Russ is best known for portraying the vulcan Tuvok in Star: Trek Voyager, what other alien race did he bring to life in a 1993 episode of Deep Space Nine called "Invasive Procedures?"

Answer: Klingon

2. What was the name of the captain of the Enterprise NX-01 depicted by Scott Bakula in the controversial Star Trek: Enterprise.

Answer: Jonathan Archer

3. What is 7 of 9's full borg designation?

Answer: 7 of 9, Tertiary Adjunct of Unimatrix Zero-One

4. What was the first name of Gene Roddenberry's wife who also played Nurse Christine Chapel in TOS and Lwaxana Troi and the voice of the ship's computer in TNG and other series?

Answer: Majel

5. What set of guidelines do Ferengi business people hold dear?

Answer: The Rules of Acquisition
